# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
People working or studying who need background music with ambient noises. They use it as a seamless background tab, or as a full-screen display on a secondary or small monitor to show the time and a nice animated wallpaper. They value Discord integration to share their listening status with friends.

## Product Purpose
A premium lofi player web app providing immersive visual themes, ambient sound mixing, and seamless YouTube/SoundCloud playback. It serves as a calm, distraction-free environment for focus.

## Positioning
A seamless, distraction-free ambient workspace in your browser.

## Brand Personality
Calm, immersive, minimal. The aesthetic draws direct inspiration from Discord's LoFi Activity: sleek, dark-mode default, deeply atmospheric, and playful yet understated.

## Anti-references
Corporate dashboards, bright white themes, overly busy or cluttered UIs, and generic Bootstrap/Tailwind templates.

## Design Principles
- **Immersive First**: The visual theme and ambient experience take priority. UI should recede when not actively in use.
- **Distraction-Free**: Minimize cognitive load. Controls should be intuitive and out of the way.
- **Ambient Utility**: Function beautifully both as an active workspace tool and as a passive secondary-monitor display (showing time and wallpaper).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard WCAG compliance (keyboard navigable, good contrast) with explicit support for reduced motion (important due to particle animations).
